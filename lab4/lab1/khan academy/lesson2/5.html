The coolest creature

Parrots are intelligent, social birds (order Psittaciformes) known for their vibrant plumage, hooked beaks, and ability to mimic sounds. With roughly 400 species, they inhabit tropical regions, using zygodactyl feet (two toes forward, two back) to climb and eat seeds, fruits, and nuts. They are popular pets, but many species are endangered.
