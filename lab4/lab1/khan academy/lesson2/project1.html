Travel to London!

London is the capital and largest city of both England and the United Kingdom, serving as a global hub for culture, finance, and history with a population of over 9 million. Situated on the River Thames in southeast England, it is a 2,000-year-old city known for landmarks like Big Ben, the Tower Bridge, and its diverse, multicultural population.

Top 3 things to see in London


  • Big Ben
  • British Museum
  • Tower Bridge

Key Facts About London

  • Location: Southeastern England on the River Thames.
  • Significance: A leading global city for finance, education, and culture.
  • Key Sites: Big Ben, Buckingham Palace, Tower Bridge, London Eye, Westminster Abbey, and British Museum.
  • Transportation: Known for its London Underground (Tube), the world's oldest, and red double-decker buses.

London is known for its diverse population, with over 300 languages spoken, and it is one of the most-visited cities in the world.
