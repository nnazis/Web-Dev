Recipe #1

  • Time: 35 min
  • Serves: 6 member
Ingredients Quantity
Dough, sausages, sauce, cheese High quantity

Step 1: Preheat oven to 220°C / 425°F.
Step 2: Prepare pizza dough and sauce.
Step 3: Add cheese.
Step 4: Place pepperoni slices evenly on top.
Step 5: Bake for 10–15 minutes until cheese melts and pepperoni crisps.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 40 min
  • Serves: 6 member
Ingredients Quantity
Dough, sausages, cheese Very tasty

Step 1: Preheat oven to 200°C / 400°F.
Step 2: Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
Step 3: Place pepperoni slices flat (not overlapping).
Step 4: Bake 8–10 minutes.
Step 5: Cool for 2 minutes.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 6 member
Ingredients Quantity
Dough, sausages, cheese Best recipe for big family

Step 1: Slice the pepperoni (if not pre-sliced).
Step 2: Heat a pan on medium heat (no oil needed).
Step 3: Place pepperoni slices in the pan.
Step 4: Cook for 1–2 minutes per side until curled and crispy.
